About the Firm

Sparsh Saxena & Co. — Cost Accountants

A proprietorship firm registered with the Institute of Cost Accountants of India under Reg. No. 006322, constituted on 11th October 2023. Head office in Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh, serving clients across India.

We serve a diverse clientele including corporates, banks, government departments, and individual businesses — bringing expertise across auditing, direct and indirect taxation, corporate law, insolvency, and project finance.

The firm has worked with the GST Department on ITC utilization certifications, assisted in Income Tax scrutiny cases, conducted revenue audits across 24+ locations, and provides ongoing consultancy in Cost Records, ESIC/EPFO, and GST compliance.

He provides consultancy in Income Tax, GST, ESIC-EPFO compliances, and handles complex Cost Audit assignments and maintenance of Cost Records. He has significant experience in Company Law matters — incorporations, secretarial assignments, compounding cases, and registration of foreign companies.

His expertise extends to Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code 2016 assignments, project appraisals, rehabilitation consulting, due diligence for M&A, and revenue auditing at 24+ locations. Also experienced in term loans, equipment finance, leasing, LCs & BGs, and OD & CC limits.
